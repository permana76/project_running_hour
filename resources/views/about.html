RUNNINGHOUR

Runninghour Co-operative Limited - 合作社

Runninghour is a sports co-operative that promotes integration of persons with special needs through running. We have members who are mildly intellectually challenged, physically challenged and visually challenged joining us to keep fit. We welcome anyone passionate about running to join us as running guides.

1. Runninghour is a co-operative which is a structure social enterprise and not a charity.

2. Runninghour's social mission is integrating people with special needs via mainstream running.

3. Runninghour's business is event management and conducting disability awareness workshops and activities.

4. Runninghour do not issue dividends to members and all revenues are used to fund operation and programmes to support our social mission.

5. Runninghour currently has a full-time staff and a volunteer management commitee.

6. Runninghour's account is audited externally and submitted to Registrar of Co-operative and Singapore National Co-operative Federation yearly.

Background of How Runninghour was Formed

Runninghour has been supported by Central Singapore Community Development Council (CDC) as a Healthy Lifestyle Club (HLC).

The inclusive running club was formed on 18th of April 2009 by a group of fitness enthusiasts. They act as buddy runners to special needs members who are mildly intellectually challenged every Saturday morning. The members are mainly students and ex-students with APSN. On 9th June 2012, a group of visually challenged runners joined Runninghour.
