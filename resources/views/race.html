INTEGRATION WORKSHOPS

We will be conducting integration workshops to raise awareness for guiding people with special needs to participate in mainstream sports activities. "Sole Mates" is a programme supported by Central Singapore CDC to promote the integration of special needs individuals with the community through sports. "Sole Mates" promote the relationship of buddies/soulmates where friendship is forged and deepened through sports activities between a special need participant and his/her sports partner.

Participants of the workshop can choose to partner a person with special needs on race day.

You could have the option to grab our past event t-shirt for just $10! Do support by attending our Integration Workshop for FREE so that you could know more about guiding person with special needs!

SPONSORSHIP

Run for Inclusion 2020 is an inaugural national event that aims to use mainstream sports as a platform to promote integration and nurture an inclusive Singapore. Run for Inclusion 2020 is a race in Singapore where participants run along-side over 500 visually, intellectually, hearing, and physically-challenged runners.

The Run will be a rallying call for Singaporeans to come together and show their support towards integrating people with special needs.

Registration

  1. What is the mode of registration and payment?
    All registrations have to be done online. Payment can only be made online via VISA or MasterCard. For special corporate request or Person With Special Needs and caregivers, please write to raceinfo@runninghour.com
  2. When is the closing date for registration?
    TBC
  3. How do I know if my registration is successful?
    Upon successful payment and registration, participants will receive a confirmation slip via email. Please check your spam or junk inbox should you not receive it in your normal inbox.
  4. What if I do not receive my confirmation slip after my registration?
    This could be due to a variety of reasons as follows:
    4.1 Your registration was not successful and therefore the confirmation slip was not rendered
    4.2 There could be an error in your email at the point of registration so the confirmation slip was unable to be sent to the correct email address.
    4.2 The email has been clocked by your mail server and/or is treated as spam.
    4.3 You can contact us at raceinfo@runninghour.com
  5. Can I get a refund if I choose not to participate after submitting my registration?
    No. All successful registration are non-refundable.
  6. Can I amend my particulars after submitting my registration?
    Yes. Participants are allowed to amend their particulars. Please send an email to raceinfo@runninghour.com to inform the organiser about the necessary changes.
  7. Can I request to change the size of the running t-shirt during the Race Entry Pack Collection?
    No. We seek your understanding that changing of sizes will not be entertained once your registration details are submitted and confirmed. Do refer to the size chart before ordering. Thank you.
  8. How do I register as a group?
    In order to register as a group, you need to gather at least 20 participants or more. Please email to raceinfo@runninghour.com for more information.
